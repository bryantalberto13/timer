        Crash Prevention

        Here are some additional tips for driving defensively to prevent crashes:

        • Make safe decisions and act on them promptly

        • Drive carefully

        • Compensate for mistakes made by other drivers or pedestrians

        • Anticipate and react appropriately to changing weather and road conditions

        • Concede the right of way if necessary to prevent a crash

        • Recognize a situation that could lead to a crash far enough in advance to prevent it

        • Wear your seat belt and keep both hands on the wheel

        • Cover the brake
